My name is Yihan Wu. I am a second-year Ph.D. student in the [Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence](http://ai.ruc.edu.cn/), [Renmin University of China](https://www.ruc.edu.cn/). My advisor is Prof. [Ruihua Song](https://gsai.ruc.edu.cn/addons/teacher/index/info.html?user_id=0&ruccode=X20HPltoADRSNQg5AWUGMQ%3D%3D&ln=cn). I am interested in Speech Synthesis, Natural Language Processing and Multimodal Learning.

Before coming to Renmin University of China, I received my B.S degrees from Shandong University in 2021. I worked at Microsoft Research Asia as a research intern with [Xu Tan](https://tan-xu.github.io/) from 2021 to 2022.
